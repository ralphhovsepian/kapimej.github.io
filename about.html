About Us

Welcome to Kapimej! We’re dedicated to making mobile connectivity accessible and easy for Armenians traveling abroad. Kapimej simplifies the way you stay connected by providing eSIM options that eliminate the need for physical SIM cards and lengthy setups.

The eSIM Solution

With Kapimej, you can purchase and activate an eSIM in just a few taps, granting you instant access to international networks. eSIMs bring a new era of convenience:

  • Easy Access: Buy an eSIM for foreign countries directly from your phone.
  • Quick Setup: No need to visit physical stores or manage multiple SIM cards.
  • Flexible Plans: Choose from data plans that fit your travel needs, whether it’s for a weekend getaway or a month-long adventure.

Our app is designed with simplicity in mind, so you can focus on enjoying your journey while we handle the connectivity.

Our Mission

At Kapimej, our mission is to empower travelers with reliable, instant connectivity. We strive to make travel easier by giving users the freedom to access mobile networks without the usual challenges of purchasing and setting up SIM cards in new countries. We are continually expanding our services and partnerships to provide you with a growing network of options, all while offering a user-centered experience.
